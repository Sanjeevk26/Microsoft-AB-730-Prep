# Using Microsoft 365 Copilot for Meetings and Collaboration

## 1. Overview

Microsoft 365 Copilot enhances meetings in Microsoft Teams by providing:

- Real-time summaries
- Insights and suggestions
- Post-meeting recaps
- Content rewriting and formatting

It helps improve productivity, collaboration, and decision-making during and after meetings.

---

## 2. Creating and Joining a Meeting

### Steps:

1. Open **Microsoft Teams**
2. Go to **Calendar**
3. Click **New Meeting**
4. Add meeting details (e.g., "AB 730 Meeting")
5. Enable **Teams Meeting**
6. Click **Save**
7. Click **Join**
8. Select:
   - Microphone
   - Speakers
9. Click **Join Now**

---

## 3. Enabling Copilot in Meetings

### Requirement

- **Transcription must be enabled**

---

### Steps to Enable Transcription

1. Click **Copilot** in the meeting toolbar  
   OR  
2. Go to:
   - More options → Record and transcribe → Start transcription  
3. Select language  
4. Click **Confirm**

---

## 4. Using Copilot During Meetings

Once transcription is active, Copilot can be used for:

---

### 4.1 Meeting Summary

Example prompt:

"Recap the meeting so far"

---

### 4.2 Insights and Analysis

You can ask:

- "Where do we disagree?"
- "What ideas have been discussed?"
- "What questions remain unresolved?"

---

### 4.3 Meeting Continuation Support

Example:

"What questions can I ask to continue the meeting?"

---

### 4.4 Output Options

- Copy responses
- Open responses in:
  - Microsoft Word
  - Microsoft Excel

Note:
Availability depends on sensitivity labels applied to the meeting.

---

## 5. Meeting Chat with Copilot

During the meeting, users can interact via chat.

---

### Features

- Standard chat messaging
- Formatting options
- Emojis, GIFs, and stickers
- Loop components
- Copilot-assisted rewriting

---

### 5.1 Rewrite with Copilot

Options include:

- Rewrite text
- Adjust tone:
  - Casual
  - Professional
  - Confident
  - Enthusiastic
- Adjust length:
  - Concise
  - Expanded

---

## 6. Ending the Meeting

- Click **Leave** to end the session

---

## 7. Post-Meeting Features

### 7.1 Meeting Chat Access

- Go to **Chat**
- Open the meeting conversation

---

### 7.2 Recap Tab

Provides:

- AI-generated summary
- Key discussion points

Note:
- Recap may not be available for very short meetings (e.g., under 5 minutes)

---

### 7.3 View Recap

- Open detailed recap in a separate window

Includes:

- Summary
- Mentions
- Transcript
- Notes

---

## 8. Audio Recap

Copilot can generate an audio summary of meetings.

---

### Features

- Podcast-style playback
- Supports:
  - Single meeting
  - Multiple meetings (last 24 hours to 30 days)

---

### Format Options

- Executive (two speakers)
- News-style (single speaker)
- Fast and concise
- Casual (two speakers, conversational)

---

## 9. Key Requirements

- Transcription must be enabled
- Sufficient meeting duration is required for summaries
- Sensitivity labels may restrict output usage

---

## 10. Use Cases

- Meeting summarization
- Task assignment tracking
- Identifying action items
- Collaboration enhancement
- Post-meeting documentation
