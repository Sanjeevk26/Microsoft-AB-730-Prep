# Managing Conversations in Microsoft 365 Copilot

## 1. Overview

Microsoft 365 Copilot allows users to manage their conversations efficiently. Users can:

- View previous conversations
- Search and filter chats
- Rename conversations
- Delete conversations
- Use temporary chats
- Manage activity history

---

## 2. Viewing Conversations

### 2.1 Conversations Panel

- Located on the left-hand side
- Displays recent conversations (typically last five)
- Shows unread notifications (e.g., scheduled prompts)

---

### 2.2 All Conversations

- Click **All Conversations** to view complete history
- Displays all saved chats within retention period

---

### 2.3 Scheduled Conversations

- Identified by a checkmark indicator
- Represents prompts that were scheduled and executed

---

## 3. Searching and Filtering Conversations

### 3.1 Search Functionality

- Use the search bar to find specific conversations
- Example: Searching for "AB 730"

---

### 3.2 Filters

Users can filter conversations by:

- All conversations
- Conversations created by specific agents
- Conversations converted into pages

---

## 4. Conversation Retention

- Copilot retains conversations for up to **18 months**
- Applies when the user is signed in

---

## 5. Temporary Chats

### 5.1 What is a Temporary Chat

- A chat session that is not saved in conversation history

---

### 5.2 Behavior

- Responses are generated normally
- Conversation does not appear in:
  - Recent chats
  - All conversations list

---

## 6. Renaming Conversations

### Steps:

1. Locate the conversation
2. Click the **three dots (ellipsis)**
3. Select **Rename**
4. Enter a new name

---

### Notes

- Useful for organizing similar conversations
- Helps differentiate chats with similar content

---

## 7. Deleting Conversations

### Steps:

1. Locate the conversation
2. Click the **three dots (ellipsis)**
3. Select **Delete**

---

### Result

- Conversation is removed from history
- Cannot be recovered

---

## 8. Managing Activity History

### 8.1 Accessing Activity Settings

1. Go to: **myaccount.microsoft.com**
2. Navigate to:
   - Account Settings
   - Privacy tab

---

### 8.2 Available Options

- View Copilot activity history
- View Microsoft search history
- Delete entire activity history

---

### 8.3 Limitations

- Cannot delete individual conversations from this portal
- Must delete conversations individually within Copilot

---

## 9. Business vs Personal Users

### Business Users

- Cannot download activity history
- Restriction exists for data protection and privacy

---

### Personal Users

- Can download activity history
- More control over personal data

---

## 10. Administrative Controls

Administrators can:

- Disable Copilot entirely
- Apply retention policies using Microsoft Purview

---

### 10.1 Retention Policies

Policies can:

- Retain conversations for a defined period (e.g., 1 day, 7 days, years)
- Automatically delete conversations after retention period
- Archive conversations for compliance purposes

---

### 10.2 Important Note

- Admins cannot manually delete individual user conversations
- They control retention, not direct deletion

---
