# Sharing Prompts in Microsoft 365 Copilot

## 1. Overview

Microsoft 365 Copilot allows users to share prompts with others within the organization. Sharing enables collaboration, reuse of prompts, and standardization of workflows.

Prompts can be shared:
- As a link
- Along with responses
- With Microsoft Teams

---

## 2. Methods to Share Prompts

### 2.1 From the Prompt (Top Section)

- Click **Copy prompt link**
- Generates a shareable link
- Can be pasted into:
  - Microsoft Teams
  - OneNote
  - Other collaboration tools

---

### 2.2 From the Response (Bottom Section)

- Click **Share Prompt and Copy with response**
- Shares both:
  - Prompt
  - Response

---

### 2.3 From Copilot Prompt Gallery

Steps:
1. Open **Copilot Prompt Gallery**
2. Go to **Your Prompts**
3. Click **Share Prompt**

Options:
- Copy link
- Share to team

---

## 3. Shared Prompt Links

All sharing methods generate a **shared prompt link**.

### Characteristics

- Accessible to users within the organization
- Can be reused by others
- Supports collaboration and knowledge sharing

---

## 4. Managing Shared Links

### 4.1 Manage Access

Users can manage shared prompts by:

- Clicking **Manage Access**
- Viewing all created shared links
- Tracking shared prompt access

---

### 4.2 Access via Settings

Steps:
1. Open Copilot Prompt Gallery
2. Click the **settings (wheel) icon**
3. View list of shared prompts

---

### 4.3 Stop Sharing

- Select a shared prompt
- Click **Stop Sharing**
- Removes access to the link

---

## 5. Sharing Prompts with Teams

### 5.1 Share to Team

Steps:
1. Click **Share Prompt**
2. Select **Share to Team**
3. Choose a Microsoft Teams team
4. Click **Share**

---

### 5.2 Team Prompts

After sharing:

- Prompts appear under **Team Prompts**
- Accessible to team members
- Enables team-level collaboration

---

### 5.3 Managing Team Prompts

Options available:

- Remove prompt from team
- Switch between different teams
- View shared prompts per team

---

## 6. Key Differences: Link Sharing vs Team Sharing

| Feature            | Link Sharing                  | Team Sharing                |
|--------------------|------------------------------|-----------------------------|
| Access Scope       | Organization-wide            | Specific team               |
| Distribution       | Manual (via link)            | Automatic (within team)     |
| Visibility         | Anyone with link             | Team members only           |
| Management         | Manage Access                | Team Prompts section        |

---

## 7. Best Practices

- Use link sharing for broad organizational use
- Use team sharing for focused collaboration
- Regularly review shared links in Manage Access
- Remove unused or outdated shared prompts
- Share prompts with responses when context is important

---

## 8. Key Takeaways

- Prompts can be shared in multiple ways
- Shared links are accessible within the organization
- Manage Access allows control over shared prompts
- Team sharing enables structured collaboration
- Prompt sharing improves efficiency and reuse
